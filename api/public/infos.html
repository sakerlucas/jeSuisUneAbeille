Infos

The aim of the game is to scan the different flowers in the botanical garden, by scanning these flowers you will get pollen and nectar which you will store in the hive. The hive will give you honey and the winners will be those who have collected the most honey.

To scan the flowers, you have to scan the QR code corresponding to each flower. Each flower is different and has different characteristics.
